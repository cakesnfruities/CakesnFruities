Our Story

From humble beginnings to a faith-based brand of excellence

The Journey of Lábálábá

Lábálábá began its sweet journey in 2019 as Asset-Cakes, a small home-based pastry business. Through dedication, faith, and countless hours of perfecting recipes, the brand evolved and was reborn in 2022 as Cakes'n'Fruities, then renames in 2026 as Lábálábá — a name inspired by divine guidance.

What started as a passion for baking has grown into a mission to inspire and empower others through the art of pastry and faith-based entrepreneurship.

Serving Over 300 Happy Clients

Our Mission

To produce professional pastry chefs and cake artists while spreading the love of Christ through every delicious creation. We believe that excellence in baking is not just about taste, but about the purpose and passion behind each product.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of faith-driven entrepreneurs in the pastry industry, creating a community where creativity meets purpose and business meets ministry.

Meet the Founder

Iyanuoluwa Phebian

A Microbiology graduate from the prestigious University of Ilorin, Iyanuoluwa Phebian discovered her true calling in the art of pastry and cake decoration. With over 300 satisfied clients and counting, she continues to build a legacy of excellence.

Her journey is a testament to how faith, education, and passion can create something truly beautiful. Beyond creating delicious treats, Phebian is passionate about mentoring the next generation of pastry chefs through her eBook and comprehensive mentorship program.
